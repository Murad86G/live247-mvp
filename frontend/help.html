Help & User Guide

Welcome to Live247 — your tool for running continuous YouTube livestreams effortlessly. This guide will help you understand how to set up and manage your streams.

1. Getting Started

  • Log in to your server (VDS) where Live247 is installed.
  • Open http://localhost:3000 (or your domain/IP if deployed).
  • Use the navigation bar to explore features.

2. Uploading Videos

  • Go to the Upload section.
  • Select one or more MP4 files (pre-rendered, recommended format).
  • Uploaded files will be available for streaming.

3. Managing Streams

  • Select a video from your library.
  • Enter your YouTube Stream Key.
  • Click Start Stream — Live247 will broadcast your video 24/7.
  • You can stop or switch streams at any time from the dashboard.

4. Settings

  • Adjust resolution, bitrate, and encoding options.
  • Set looping options for continuous playback.
  • Manage multiple channels if needed.

5. Troubleshooting

  • If the stream doesn’t start, double-check your YouTube Stream Key.
  • Make sure your server has enough CPU/RAM to handle encoding.
  • Check logs for errors in server.js.

6. Support

If you need further assistance, contact our support team or check the FAQ section.
